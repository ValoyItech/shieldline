About Shield-Line Security

Protecting Boksburg and surrounding areas with professional security services since day one. Your safety is our commitment.

Our Story

Shield-Line Security was founded with a simple yet powerful mission: to provide world-class security services that give our clients peace of mind. What started as a small team of dedicated security professionals has grown into one of Boksburg's most trusted security providers.

Our team brings together years of experience from military, law enforcement, and private security backgrounds. We understand that security is not just about having guards at the door—it's about creating comprehensive protection strategies tailored to each client's unique needs.

Today, we serve residential properties, businesses, events, and VIP clients throughout the region, maintaining our commitment to excellence and rapid response that our clients depend on 24/7.
